01 — About

From ledgers to product logs

Six years across banking and agri-manufacturing, most of it spent noticing the same problem in different clothes: good decisions buried under manual tracking, tribal knowledge, and slow feedback loops. At HDFC that meant advising clients through insurance and lending products; at ARI it meant building the tools myself when nothing off-the-shelf fit a two-facility rice mill's procurement cycle.

The BITSoM program formalized what I'd already started doing — treating problems as products, and AI as a build material rather than a buzzword. Samjhdari Suraksha, the WhatsApp insurance advisor, is where those two threads meet: BFSI domain depth plus hands-on agentic architecture.
